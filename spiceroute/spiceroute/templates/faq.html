Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our platform.

We partner with local Micro-Supply Hubs (MSHs) who are vetted by our platform. These MSHs are trained to perform standardized quality checks before delivering goods to you. Additionally, our review system allows vendors like you to provide feedback, helping us maintain a high standard of quality and trust.

Our AI analyzes your past purchase history, seasonal trends, and local market data to recommend products and quantities. For suppliers, it suggests optimal pricing based on local competition and demand forecasts. This helps both sides of the market make smarter, more profitable decisions.

Our micro-loan system is designed to provide quick, short-term capital for vendors to manage their daily cash flow. You can apply directly from your dashboard by specifying the amount and a flexible repayment period (e.g., 2 days, 1 week). Loan applications are subject to a quick admin review.

If you and a fellow vendor are placing a combined order, you can enter your co-vendor's ID (phone number or email) during checkout. This allows you both to benefit from bulk pricing and a small extra discount, fostering community and collaboration.

On your dashboard, navigate to the "Leftover Market" section. You can list your healthy, unused items for sale at a lower price. Other vendors in your area can then browse and contact you directly to purchase, helping you reduce food waste and recoup costs.

SpiceRoute operates on a small service fee per transaction to cover platform maintenance, logistics support, and AI-powered features. We are committed to keeping these fees low to ensure our platform remains accessible and beneficial for all vendors and suppliers.

Our platform tracks delivery times and supplier performance. If an order is late or you receive a sub-standard product, you can report the issue through our support channel. Your reviews and feedback are crucial for holding MSHs accountable and ensuring a reliable service for the entire community.

Yes! Our platform supports voice-based search in multiple Indian languages, including Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, and Bengali. Simply click the microphone icon in the search bar, select your language, and speak your query.

A Micro-Supply Hub is a local entrepreneur or small business that acts as an aggregation and distribution point for raw materials. To become an MSH, you can register through our platform's sign-up page and undergo a simple vetting process to ensure you meet our quality and service standards.

Once submitted, your loan application goes into a 'pending' state, awaiting review and approval by a platform administrator. You can track the status of your application on your "My Loans" page. If approved, the funds will be disbursed, and the loan status will change to 'active'.
