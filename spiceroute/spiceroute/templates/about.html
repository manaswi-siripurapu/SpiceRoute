About SpiceRoute

Our mission is to empower India's street food vendors by solving the hidden challenges of raw material sourcing.

The Problem We're Solving

Every day, millions of street food vendors across India face a fragmented and unreliable supply chain. They struggle with inconsistent quality, volatile pricing, and a massive time drain spent sourcing fresh ingredients. This directly impacts their profitability, food safety, and ability to grow their business.

Our Solution

SpiceRoute is a digital platform designed to create a transparent, efficient, and trusted ecosystem. We connect vendors with local Micro-Supply Hubs (MSHs), enabling collective purchasing, quality assurance, and streamlined logistics. Our platform uses AI to provide smart insights, helping both vendors and suppliers make better decisions.

Building Trust

Through review-based authentication and transparent transactions, we're building a network of trust from farm to table.

Ensuring Quality

We empower MSHs to perform standardized quality checks, ensuring vendors get the best ingredients every time.

Financial Inclusion

Our micro-loan system provides vendors with the necessary capital to scale their operations and overcome daily cash flow challenges.
